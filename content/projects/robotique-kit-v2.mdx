## Project Overview

A comprehensive brand evolution for Robotique, including updated logo, color palette, typography system, and complete design asset library.

## Goals

- Modernize visual identity while maintaining brand recognition
- Create scalable design system for all touchpoints
- Improve consistency across digital and physical products
- Enable faster content production with reusable components

## Status

Design exploration phase. Testing various directions with community feedback.

## Components

- Logo variations and lockups
- Color system with accessibility standards
- Typography hierarchy
- Icon library
- Pattern and texture assets
- Social media templates
- Product mockup templates

## Timeline

Expected completion: Q1 2025
