## Project Overview

Expanding the Orion's Barrel universe with standalone news segments. Each video presents "breaking news" from across the galaxy with the same comedic tone and visual style as the main series.

## Status

Currently in development. Pilot episodes are being produced to test format and audience reception.

## Planned Features

- 60-90 second news segments
- Rotating anchors and correspondents
- Visual news graphics and lower-thirds
- Serialized storylines across episodes
- Integration with main Orion's Barrel series

## Coming Soon

Full launch planned for Q4 2024 with weekly releases across social platforms.
