Our Story

Music That Refuses To Be Quiet

Just Demon Records is an independent electronic music label born from years of underground gigging, BBC Radio 1 airplay, and global festival stages. We don't follow trends — we make noise and let the music speak.


Our releases have been supported by icons including Moby, Leeroy Thornhill of The Prodigy, High Contrast, Annie Nightingale, and Dave Spoon. We've had music played at Tomorrowland and Ultra Music Festival. Now we're back — releasing what we love, on our terms.
